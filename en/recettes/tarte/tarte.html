La Tarte au Libouli
Libouli pie is a delicious
specialty of Boulonnais, a flan
traditionally cooked over a wood fire.
It is usually topped with prunes,
offering a unique combination of flavors
sweet and a creamy texture that
delights the taste buds of
regional cuisine.
INGREDIENTS FOR 4 PEOPLE
  • 250 g sugar
  • 80 g flour
  • salt
  • 1 l milk (set aside 1 cup per side)
  • 3 eggs
STEP 1
  • Prepare a dough.
STEP 2
  • Dissolve the yeast in the milk with the sugar.
STEP 3
  • Add to flour with other ingredients, knead to form an elastic ball.
  • Let stand for 2 hours.
STEP 4
  • Roll out the dough and divide into 3 balls.
  • Let stand for 1 hour.
STEP 5
  • Prepare a libouli.
  • For those who don’t speak patois, boiled milk!
STEP 6
  • Boil the milk with half the sugar.
  • Beat the eggs with the other half of the sugar, flour, remaining cup of milk and salt.
STEP 7
  • Add to boiling milk and thicken over low heat, stirring, stopping 3 minutes after boiling.
STEP 8
  • Spread the pie dough and pour the libouli.
  • Bake in thermostat 7 or 8 oven for 45 minutes.
  • The top should then be brown and yellow.
  • If the color is not reached, continue cooking.
